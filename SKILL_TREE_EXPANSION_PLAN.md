## A1 (Absolute Beginner) - 18 Units | 72 Lessons

### Unit 1: First Words
- **Topics**: Greetings, basic expressions (hello, goodbye, yes, no, please, thank you)
- **Grammar**: Basic greetings, polite expressions
- **Vocabulary**: Essential survival words

### Unit 2: Introducing Yourself
- **Topics**: Name, nationality, age
- **Grammar**: "Me llamo...", "Soy de...", "Tengo X años"
- **Vocabulary**: Personal information basics

### Unit 3: Numbers 0-20
- **Topics**: Counting, basic math
- **Grammar**: Number agreement
- **Vocabulary**: Numbers 0-20

### Unit 4: Numbers 21-100
- **Topics**: Larger numbers, prices, ages
- **Grammar**: Number patterns (veinte, treinta, etc.)
- **Vocabulary**: Numbers 21-100

### Unit 5: Days of the Week
- **Topics**: Days, schedules
- **Grammar**: Days with articles
- **Vocabulary**: lunes, martes, miércoles, jueves, viernes, sábado, domingo

### Unit 6: Months & Dates
- **Topics**: Calendar, birthdays, appointments
- **Grammar**: Date expressions
- **Vocabulary**: 12 months, date phrases

### Unit 7: Telling Time
- **Topics**: Clock time, schedules
- **Grammar**: "¿Qué hora es?", "Es la/Son las..."
- **Vocabulary**: Time expressions

### Unit 8: Family Members
- **Topics**: Family relationships
- **Grammar**: Possessive adjectives (mi, tu, su)
- **Vocabulary**: padre, madre, hermano, hermana, abuelo, abuela, etc.

### Unit 9: Colors & Shapes
- **Topics**: Colors, basic shapes
- **Grammar**: Color adjective agreement
- **Vocabulary**: rojo, azul, verde, amarillo, negro, blanco, etc.

### Unit 10: Food & Drinks
- **Topics**: Basic foods, meals
- **Grammar**: Articles with food
- **Vocabulary**: agua, pan, carne, fruta, verduras, etc.

### Unit 11: At the Restaurant
- **Topics**: Ordering food, restaurant phrases
- **Grammar**: "Quisiera...", "Me gustaría...", "La cuenta, por favor"
- **Vocabulary**: Restaurant vocabulary

### Unit 12: Common Objects
- **Topics**: Everyday items
- **Grammar**: Articles and plurals
- **Vocabulary**: mesa, silla, libro, teléfono, etc.

### Unit 13: In the House
- **Topics**: Rooms, furniture
- **Grammar**: Location prepositions (en, sobre, debajo)
- **Vocabulary**: cocina, dormitorio, baño, sala, etc.

### Unit 14: Clothing
- **Topics**: What you wear
- **Grammar**: "Llevar" verb, clothing descriptions
- **Vocabulary**: camisa, pantalón, zapatos, vestido, etc.

### Unit 15: Daily Routine
- **Topics**: Daily activities, routines
- **Grammar**: Reflexive verbs (levantarse, ducharse)
- **Vocabulary**: despertarse, desayunar, trabajar, dormir

### Unit 16: Weather
- **Topics**: Weather conditions, seasons
- **Grammar**: "Hace..." weather expressions
- **Vocabulary**: sol, lluvia, nieve, viento, nublado

### Unit 17: Likes & Dislikes
- **Topics**: Preferences, opinions
- **Grammar**: Gustar verb (me gusta, me gustan)
- **Vocabulary**: Preference expressions

### Unit 18: Basic Questions
- **Topics**: Question formation
- **Grammar**: Question words (quién, qué, dónde, cuándo, por qué, cómo)
- **Vocabulary**: Question phrases
